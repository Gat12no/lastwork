Shakam Paa

Dried beef stir-fry with radish and chili. Serves 4 • Medium • ~1 hour 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 250g dried beef (shakam)
  • 2 medium daikon radishes, sliced
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 5-6 dried red chilies
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, julienned
  • 3 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tsp Sichuan peppercorns (optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare Beef: Soak dried beef in warm water for 30 minutes to soften. Drain and cut into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Sauté Aromatics: Heat oil in a wok or large pan. Add onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add Spices: Add garlic, ginger, and dried chilies. Stir-fry for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Cook Beef: Add the soaked beef and stir-fry for 5-7 minutes until slightly browned.
  5. Add Radish: Add sliced daikon radish and mix well.
  6. Simmer: Add water, salt, and Sichuan peppercorns. Cover and simmer for 30-40 minutes until beef is tender.
  7. Final Touch: Uncover and cook until most liquid has evaporated, leaving a thick sauce.
  8. Serve hot with red rice.
