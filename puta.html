Puta (Buckwheat Noodles)

Traditional buckwheat noodles with spicy sauce. Serves 4 • Medium • ~1 hour

Ingredients

For Noodles:

  • 2 cups buckwheat flour
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 1/2 tsp salt

For Sauce:

  • 4 tbsp chili oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 tsp Sichuan peppercorns, toasted
  • 2 spring onions, chopped
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Make Dough: Mix flours and salt. Gradually add warm water to form a firm dough. Knead for 10 minutes.
  2. Rest Dough: Cover dough and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  3. Roll & Cut: Roll dough thin and cut into noodles, or use a pasta machine.
  4. Cook Noodles: Boil noodles in salted water for 3-5 minutes until cooked but firm. Drain and rinse with cold water.
  5. Prepare Sauce: Mix chili oil, garlic, soy sauce, vinegar, and crushed Sichuan peppercorns.
  6. Combine: Toss noodles with the spicy sauce until well coated.
  7. Garnish: Top with spring onions and toasted sesame seeds.
  8. Serve immediately, either warm or at room temperature.
