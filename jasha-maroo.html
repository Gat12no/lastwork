Jasha Maroo

Spicy chicken stew with tomatoes and chili. Serves 4 • Medium • ~55 minutes

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken, cut into small pieces
  • 2 large onions, finely chopped
  • 4 tomatoes, chopped
  • 5-6 green chilies, sliced
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 inch ginger, minced
  • 3 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 cups water
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Steps

  1. Prep: Clean and cut chicken into small pieces. Chop onions, tomatoes, and slice chilies.
  2. Sauté Onions: Heat oil in a pot. Add onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add Aromatics: Add garlic and ginger. Cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Chicken: Add chicken pieces and turmeric powder. Cook until chicken changes color.
  5. Tomatoes & Chilies: Add tomatoes and green chilies. Cook until tomatoes soften.
  6. Simmer: Add water and salt. Cover and simmer for 25-30 minutes until chicken is tender.
  7. Final Touch: Cook uncovered for 5 minutes to thicken the sauce if needed.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with rice.
