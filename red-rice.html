Red Rice

Traditional Bhutanese red rice preparation. Serves 4 • Easy • ~30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Bhutanese red rice
  • 3 cups water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp butter or oil (optional)

Note: Bhutanese red rice has a nutty flavor and slightly chewy texture. Rinsing twice helps achieve the perfect consistency.

Steps

  1. Rinse Rice: Place red rice in a bowl and rinse under cold water 2-3 times until water runs clear.
  2. Soak (Optional): For best results, soak rice in water for 20-30 minutes before cooking.
  3. Cook: In a pot, combine rinsed rice, water, salt, and butter/oil if using.
  4. Bring to Boil: Bring to a rolling boil over high heat.
  5. Simmer: Once boiling, reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes.
  6. Rest: Remove from heat and let it sit covered for 5-10 minutes.
  7. Fluff: Fluff rice with a fork before serving.
  8. Serve as a base for curries, stews, or with any Bhutanese main dish.
