Hoentay

Buckwheat dumplings filled with turnip greens. Serves 4 • Medium • ~1 hour 15 minutes

Ingredients

For Dough:

  • 2 cups buckwheat flour
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1/2 tsp salt

For Filling:

  • 3 cups turnip greens, finely chopped
  • 1 cup cheese (datshi or feta), crumbled
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Prepare Filling: Heat oil and sauté onions until soft. Add garlic, then turnip greens. Cook until wilted. Mix in cheese and season. Let cool.
  2. Make Dough: Mix flours and salt. Gradually add warm water to form a smooth dough. Knead for 5 minutes.
  3. Divide Dough: Divide dough into small balls (about 20). Roll each into a thin circle.
  4. Fill Dumplings: Place 1 tbsp filling in center of each circle. Fold and seal edges tightly.
  5. Steam: Arrange dumplings in steamer. Steam for 15-20 minutes until cooked through.
  6. Pan-fry (Optional): For crispy hoentay, pan-fry steamed dumplings in butter until golden.
  7. Serve hot with ezay (chili relish) or tomato chutney.
