Ezay (Chili Relish)

Spicy chili condiment to accompany any meal. Makes 1 cup • Easy • ~15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 10-12 fresh red chilies
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 inch ginger
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp Sichuan peppercorns (optional)
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Tip: Adjust the number of chilies based on your spice tolerance. For milder ezay, remove seeds from chilies.

Steps

  1. Prep Ingredients: Roughly chop chilies, garlic, ginger, onion, and tomatoes.
  2. Roast (Optional): For deeper flavor, briefly roast chilies, garlic, and ginger in a dry pan.
  3. Blend: Combine all ingredients except oil in a blender or mortar. Pulse until coarsely chopped.
  4. Cook: Heat oil in a pan. Add the blended mixture and cook for 5-7 minutes on medium heat.
  5. Season: Add salt and Sichuan peppercorns if using. Cook for another 2 minutes.
  6. Cool: Remove from heat and let it cool completely.
  7. Garnish: Mix in fresh coriander.
  8. Store in an airtight container in refrigerator for up to 2 weeks.
